ABOUT
The Wave House is Boston's Premiere French New Wave Cinema House. Serving the Boston community since 1994, The Wave House is a two screen cinmea that caters to French New Wave films of the 1950's and 1960's. In addition to films, we offer a full-service dining experience and full-service bar located in the lobby (for those over 21).

Films are changed on a monthly basis.
